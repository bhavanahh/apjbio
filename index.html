Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Aerospace Scientist & 11th President of India

Dr. Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007. Known as the "Missile Man of India", he played a pivotal role in India's civilian space and missile development programs.

Awarded the Bharat Ratna, he remained committed to education, youth development, and national progress. He passed away while giving a lecture—an example of his undying dedication to learning and teaching.
